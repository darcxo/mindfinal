Self-Help Techniques

Evidence-based techniques you can try right now.

😮‍💨 Box Breathing (for anxiety & panic)

Activates your parasympathetic nervous system and calms you in under 2 minutes.

  • 1.Breathe IN slowly for 4 counts
  • 2.HOLD your breath for 4 counts
  • 3.Breathe OUT slowly for 4 counts
  • 4.HOLD again for 4 counts. Repeat 4–6 times.

🖐️ 5-4-3-2-1 Grounding (for panic attacks)

Brings you back to the present moment when anxiety spirals out of control.

  • 5things you can SEE around you
  • 4things you can TOUCH
  • 3things you can HEAR
  • 2things you can SMELL
  • 1thing you can TASTE

📝 Journaling (for overthinking & low mood)

Writing externalizes your thoughts — your brain stops looping because it feels 'heard'.

  • →Write 5–10 minutes without stopping or editing
  • →Try: "Right now I feel ___ because ___. What I need is ___."
  • →MindBridge check-in notes count as journaling!

⏱️ Pomodoro Technique (for exam stress)

Breaks study into manageable chunks. Your brain retains 40% more in focused sessions.

  • 1.Set a timer for 25 minutes — study with full focus
  • 2.Take a 5-minute break — walk, stretch, breathe
  • 3.After 4 rounds, take a 20-minute longer break
